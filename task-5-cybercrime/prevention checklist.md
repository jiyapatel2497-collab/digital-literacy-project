Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
Never share OTPs or banking details with anyone
Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown emails
Always check the website URL before entering information
Enable two-factor authentication for extra security
Do not download files from untrusted sources
Financial / UPI Safety:
Always verify UPI requests before approving payments
Do not scan unknown QR codes for receiving money
Reporting Cybercrime:
Report at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930
